About

SoundCoustics is an abbreviation and a combination of the words acoustic sounds. Music can deliver emotions even without understanding the words. We can feel the emotions of a song from just the instrumental rhythm. We can feel sorrow, happiness and even the combination of these two emotions just from the beats and flow of the song. This company objective is to deliver those feelings from selling acoustics instruments, mainly guitars and ukuleles, to people so that people can not only feel those feelings from their favourite songs but also play their favourite music! We sell all kinds of acoustics guitar ranging from for professional use and for beginners who want to learn to play them!
